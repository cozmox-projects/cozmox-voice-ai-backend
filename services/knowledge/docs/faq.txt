Acme Corp — Frequently Asked Questions
========================================

Q: What are your customer support hours?
A: Our support team is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM Eastern Time.
For urgent issues, our automated system is available 24/7.

Q: How do I track my order?
A: You can track your order by logging into your account at acmecorp.com and clicking
"My Orders." You will also receive an email with a tracking link once your order ships.
Orders typically ship within 2 business days.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover),
PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and ACH bank transfers for orders over $500.

Q: Do you offer free shipping?
A: Yes. Orders over $75 qualify for free standard shipping within the United States.
Expedited shipping is available for an additional fee.

Q: How do I change or cancel my order?
A: Orders can be modified or cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After that, the order
enters processing and cannot be changed. Please contact support immediately if you need
to make changes.

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: We currently ship to the United States, Canada, the United Kingdom, and Australia.
International orders may be subject to customs duties which are the customer's responsibility.

Q: How do I reset my password?
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. You will receive
a reset link within 5 minutes. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder.

Q: Is my payment information secure?
A: Yes. All transactions are encrypted using SSL and we are PCI DSS compliant.
We never store your full credit card number on our servers.
