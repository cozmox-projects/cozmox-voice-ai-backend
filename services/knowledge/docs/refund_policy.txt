Acme Corp Refund Policy
========================

Standard Refund Window:
Customers may request a full refund within 30 days of purchase for any reason.
After 30 days and up to 90 days, a 50% store credit refund is available.
No refunds are issued after 90 days from the date of purchase.

How to Request a Refund:
Customers can request a refund by calling our support line, emailing support@acmecorp.com,
or logging into their account portal and submitting a refund request under "Order History."

Processing Time:
Refunds to the original payment method are processed within 5 to 7 business days.
Store credit refunds are applied instantly to the customer's account.

Non-Refundable Items:
Digital downloads that have been accessed are non-refundable.
Customized or personalized orders are non-refundable.
Subscription services are refunded on a prorated basis for the unused portion.

Defective Products:
If a product is defective or arrives damaged, customers are entitled to a full refund or
replacement regardless of the 30-day window. Customers must provide a photo of the defect.

Subscription Cancellation:
Customers can cancel subscriptions at any time. The subscription remains active until the
end of the current billing period. No partial refunds are given for mid-cycle cancellations
unless the product is defective.
