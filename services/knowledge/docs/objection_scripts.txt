Acme Corp — Objection Handling Guide
======================================

OBJECTION: "This is too expensive" / "I can find it cheaper elsewhere"
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Response approach: Acknowledge, then pivot to value.
Script: "I completely understand budget is a concern. Our pricing reflects the 2-year warranty
and free lifetime support included with every purchase — competitors typically charge extra
for both. Would a payment plan help? We offer 0% financing over 6 months for orders over $200."

OBJECTION: "I've been waiting too long for my order"
───────────────────────────────────────────────────
Response approach: Empathize first, then solve.
Script: "I'm really sorry for the wait — that's not the experience we want you to have.
Let me pull up your order right now. If your order is more than 3 days past the estimated
delivery date, you're eligible for a shipping refund and expedited re-ship at no cost."

OBJECTION: "Your product didn't work / broke quickly"
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Response approach: Don't be defensive. Move to resolution fast.
Script: "That's really disappointing and I apologize. You should absolutely not have had
that experience. Our products carry a 2-year warranty. I can arrange a replacement shipped
to you tomorrow, or if you prefer, a full refund — whichever you'd like."

OBJECTION: "I want to cancel my subscription"
──────────────────────────────────────────────
Response approach: Understand why before offering a solution.
Script: "Of course, I can help you with that. Before I do, could I ask what's prompting
the cancellation? Sometimes there's something we can fix or adjust. If you'd prefer to
pause rather than cancel, we can pause your subscription for up to 3 months at no charge."

OBJECTION: "I already talked to someone and they didn't help me"
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Response approach: Take ownership. Don't blame previous agent.
Script: "I'm sorry you had to call back — that's frustrating. I'm going to personally
make sure we get this resolved for you today. Can you walk me through what happened so
I have the full picture?"

OBJECTION: "I don't want to give my credit card over the phone"
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Response approach: Respect their concern, offer alternatives.
Script: "That's completely understandable and a very sensible approach. You can complete
your purchase safely on our website at acmecorp.com, or I can send you a secure payment
link via email right now — no card information shared over the phone at all."
